How to Achieve Healthy, Beautiful Hair on Wash Day

Wash day isn’t just about scrubbing your scalp—it’s a full-on spa day for your hair (minus the cucumber water). Start by giving your scalp some love with a quick massage, lather up with the right shampoo (your ends don’t need soap opera drama, just your scalp does), then slather on conditioner like it’s liquid gold. Rinse with cool water for that shiny-hair moment, blot gently with a T-shirt instead of wrestling with a towel, and lock in moisture with a leave-in. Bonus points if you skip frying your strands with heat tools—your hair will thank you by looking fabulous instead of frazzled.

The Product Do's & Donts you needed to Know

When it comes to hair products, a few do’s and don’ts go a long way. Do use the right products for your hair type—your curls don’t want the same stuff as your bestie’s poker-straight locks. Do layer lightly (a pea-sized amount of serum is not a suggestion, it’s a rule). Don’t go wild with heavy oils unless you’re into the “haven’t-showered-in-a-week” aesthetic, and definitely don’t skip a heat protectant unless crispy, fried ends are your thing. Basically: treat your hair products like seasoning—enough to enhance, not drown out.
